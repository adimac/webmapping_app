Adi McCrea

Interests

Data. Data. More data. Open data. Presently, Adi is particularly keen on the London Datastore but is also working on some data viz using house prices from the Land Registry as part of "Club Open Data" (@clubopendata on Twitter).

Adi is solid in ArcGIS and QGIS (with PostgreSQL and PostGIS) and is expanding her data viz and analysis toolkit with CartoDB, tableau and R. Adi also created the pages that you see here, including the design and implementation of the "KiLR" app.

Academic Research Interests

Adi is a GIS Masters student with research interests in geodemographic reconstructions of Dublin's post-famine landscapes. Adi will be working closely with Queen's University Centre for Economic History to geoprocess data from Kilmainham Gaol, Dublin spanning a period of almost 100 years and beginning in 1832.

Life before GIS

Adi's most recent position was as a tutor to the young cast of the feature film "Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children".

Prior to this, Adi taught Geography, organised overseas expeditions and was Head of Careers at a North London School for girls.

Adi is often found hanging from a wall at White Spider in South West London or using two wheels to navigate the Surrey Hills.
